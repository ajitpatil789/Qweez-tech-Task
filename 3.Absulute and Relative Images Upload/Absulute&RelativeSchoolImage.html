D Y Patil School Kolhapur



Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyaniketan School in Kolhapur, Maharashtra, is a CBSE-affiliated secondary school established in 2018, located at Salokhe Nagar, with affiliation number 1131052. The school is managed by the Dr. D. Y. Patil Pratishthan and offers modern educational facilities, including smart classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, music room, playground, and a school canteen. They also provide various extracurricular activities like sports, arts, and student counseling. The school follows the CBSE curriculum, with admissions open for primary through higher secondary levels, subject to CBSE guidelines. Required documents for admission include birth and health certificates, report cards, passport-sized photos, and identification for parents.
